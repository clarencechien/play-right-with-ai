# 行為準則 | Code of Conduct

## 我們的承諾 | Our Pledge

為了營造一個開放且友善的環境，我們作為貢獻者和維護者承諾，讓參與我們專案和社群的每個人都能享有無騷擾的體驗，無論其年齡、體型、身心障礙、族群、性別特徵、性別認同和表達、經驗水平、教育程度、社會經濟地位、國籍、外貌、種族、宗教或性認同和取向如何。

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 我們的標準 | Our Standards

有助於創造正面環境的行為包括：
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

* 使用友善和包容的語言 | Using welcoming and inclusive language
* 尊重不同的觀點和經驗 | Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
* 優雅地接受建設性批評 | Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
* 專注於對社群最有利的事 | Focusing on what is best for the community
* 對其他社群成員表現同理心 | Showing empathy towards other community members

不可接受的行為包括：
Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

* 使用性暗示語言或圖像，以及不受歡迎的性關注或挑逗 | The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
* 挑釁、侮辱/貶低評論，以及人身或政治攻擊 | Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* 公開或私下騷擾 | Public or private harassment
* 未經明確許可，發布他人的私人資訊，如實體或電子地址 | Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
* 其他在專業環境中合理認為不當的行為 | Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 我們的責任 | Our Responsibilities

專案維護者有責任為可接受的行為標準做出詮釋，並對任何不可接受的行為採取適當且公正的糾正措施。

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

專案維護者有權利和責任移除、編輯或拒絕與本行為準則不符的評論、提交、程式碼、wiki 編輯、問題和其他貢獻，或因其他他們認為不適當、具威脅性、冒犯性或有害的行為，暫時或永久封鎖任何貢獻者。

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## 適用範圍 | Scope

本行為準則適用於專案空間和公共空間中代表專案或其社群的個人。代表專案或社群的範例包括使用官方專案電子郵件地址、透過官方社群媒體帳號發布，或在線上或線下活動中擔任指定代表。專案維護者可能會進一步定義和澄清專案的代表。

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community. Examples of representing a project or community include using an official project e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event. Representation of a project may be further defined and clarified by project maintainers.

## 執行 | Enforcement

如遇到濫用、騷擾或其他不可接受的行為，請聯繫專案團隊 [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS]。所有投訴都將被審查和調查，並將做出必要且適當的回應。專案團隊有義務對事件報告者保密。具體執行政策的更多細節可能會單獨發布。

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident. Further details of specific enforcement policies may be posted separately.

未真誠遵守或執行行為準則的專案維護者可能會面臨由專案領導層其他成員決定的暫時或永久性後果。

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
